The Foundry Process

A repeatable method that turns “AI ideas” into stable operating leverage.

  1. Flight Readiness
    We map your workflows, identify highest-ROI automations, and define success metrics (time saved, response time, revenue lift).
  2. Wing Design
    We pick systems, access patterns, guardrails, and escalation rules. No black boxes.
  3. Build + Ship
    We implement 2–4 automations fast, test with real data, and train the team. Humans remain the final authority.
  4. Altitude Ops
    We monitor, iterate, and keep it reliable as your business changes. You get a system, not a one-off.

FAQ

Quick answers for operators who don’t have time for fluff.

Do you build custom software or use automation tools?
Both. We start with the simplest reliable path (often Zapier/Make/n8n). If your workflow needs custom code for security, scale, or performance, we build it.
Will AI replace our staff?
No. The goal is to remove repetitive admin work and improve response time. Humans stay in control—especially for approvals, money movement, and sensitive comms.
What systems do you integrate with?
Common: Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, CRMs, ticketing tools, phone systems, and property management platforms. We’ll confirm what’s possible during the Audit.
How do you measure ROI?
We set metrics up front: hours saved, time-to-response, ticket throughput, conversion rates, and error reduction. You’ll get a weekly summary during implementation.
