# Internal — Discovery Call Playbook (Close-Oriented)

## Discovery Call Structure (built to close)
**0–3 min: Frame + outcome**
- Goal: pick 1–2 workflows worth automating, confirm fit, and if it’s a fit **lock the Sprint today**.

**3–12 min: Pain + volume + urgency**
- Where is inbound crushing you?
- What’s getting dropped?
- What does a dropped ball cost (vacancy days, missed tours, overtime, angry owners)?

**12–20 min: Workflow selection (make it real)**
- Pick Workflow #1 and #2.
- Confirm systems (Outlook + PM software + any CRM/spreadsheets).
- Confirm “risk mode”: draft‑first vs auto‑send categories.

**20–26 min: Offer + pricing**
- 14‑Day AI Ops Sprint **starting at $7,500** — **2 workflows minimum** (often 2–4 depending on complexity)
- Optional add‑on: **Outbound Messaging (Email + SMS) +$3,500 flat**

**26–30 min: Close**
- Can you have access + a workflow owner ready Day 1?
- If yes: send agreement immediately and lock the start date.

---

## Verbatim-Friendly Talk Track

### Open (0–2 min)
“Appreciate you jumping on. My goal today is simple: identify the 1–2 workflows that will save you the most time/money fastest, confirm we’re a fit, and if we are, we’ll lock the Sprint today.”

### Context + pain (2–8 min)
- “What’s the #1 operational bottleneck right now?”
- “Where are requests/leads getting dropped?”
- “What happens when that breaks—lost leases, resident churn, overtime, angry owners?”

### Volume + baseline (8–12 min)
- “How many inbound items/day for maintenance? leasing? vendor?”
- “What’s your current response time on average?”
- “Who touches it today, and how many handoffs?”

### Pick workflows (12–18 min)
“Let’s pick two workflows for the Sprint.”
- Workflow #1: “If we automated only one thing in the next 14 days, what’s the best ROI?”
- Workflow #2: “What’s the second best ROI that also reduces chaos?”
Confirm systems: “You’re on Outlook, and your PM system is ____ — correct?”

### Risk mode (18–22 min)
“By default we run **draft‑first**—your team approves messages before anything goes out. If you want direct resident messaging (email/SMS), we can do that with guardrails.”
- “Are you comfortable with draft‑first only, or do you want auto‑send for specific templates like confirmations/status updates?”

### Offer + price (22–26 min)
“Here’s the offer: a 14‑Day AI Ops Sprint starting at **$7,500**. You get **2 workflows minimum** shipped into your real systems—often 2–4 depending on complexity—plus monitoring, rollback, runbooks, and training.”
“If you want **direct resident messaging (email + SMS)**, that’s an add‑on: **+$3,500 flat** because it requires extra guardrails, testing, and monitoring.”

### Close (26–30 min)
“If we start on [DATE], can you have:
1) a workflow owner available Day 1, and
2) access to the inbox + systems we need?”
“If yes, we’re good to move forward—want me to send the agreement now and lock the start date?”

If they hesitate:
“No worries—what’s the one thing you’d need to see to feel comfortable signing?”

## Post‑Discovery Follow‑Up Package
**Send within 1 hour**:
- 5‑bullet scope recap (Workflow #1/#2)
- Price: $7,500 starting + optional $3,500 messaging add‑on
- Two start date options
- Link to schedule a 10‑minute “finalize + sign” call

**Cadence:**
- T+2 hours: “Any red flags?”
- Next morning: “Start date A or B?”
- 48 hours: “Closing the loop—keep a slot open?”
