# Managed AI Ops — Care Plan (Pricing + Positioning)

## Target
Small property management companies without dedicated IT.

## Positioning
**We don’t just ship automations — we keep them healthy.**
Tools change, inbox patterns change, staff changes. Without maintenance, workflows degrade or fail quietly.

Name options:
- AI Ops Care Plan
- Workflow Maintenance + Monitoring
- Managed AI Ops

## Default recommendation
**Care+ (Standard) — $1,500/mo**

## Contract term
- **Minimum term:** 3 months
- Then month-to-month (or annual discount optional later)

---

## What’s included (core bundle)
- Client-dedicated hosted runtime (OpenClaw instance)
- Monitoring + alerting (workflow failures, API/integration issues, message delivery failures)
- Break/fix support
- Minor iteration (copy tweaks, routing rules, small logic changes)
- Security hygiene (access review, key/credential rotation cadence, updates)
- Monthly health report (runs, failures, improvements shipped, next recommendations)

**Pass-through (not included):**
- SMS/email provider usage
- Paid integrations (Zapier, etc.)
- AI/LLM API usage (if applicable)

---

## Tiers
### Tier 1 — Care (Light) — $750/mo
- Up to 2 workflows
- Monitoring + break/fix
- 1 hour minor changes/month
- Best-effort response (e.g., 1–2 business days)

### Tier 2 — Care+ (Standard) — $1,500/mo (default)
- Up to 4 workflows
- Monitoring + break/fix
- 3 hours minor changes/month
- Faster response target (next business day)
- Monthly optimization recommendations

### Tier 3 — Managed Ops (Premium) — $3,000/mo
- Up to 8 workflows
- Monitoring + break/fix
- 6 hours minor changes/month
- Priority response
- Quarterly tune-up session

---

## Optional add-ons
- Outbound Messaging Support: +$500–$1,000/mo (if heavy auto-send and compliance overhead)
- After-hours coverage: priced separately (do not include by default)

---

## Sales phrasing
“You’ve got the plane flying. The care plan is the maintenance crew — monitoring, fixes, and improvements so it doesn’t fail quietly. Most teams without IT choose Care+.”
